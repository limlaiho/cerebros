WHAT ARE WE SOLVING

Systematic approach to make sense of event data

Problem

Economic Benefits

Exhibition event organizers find it hard to justify the economic benefits participating companies could stand to reap beyond the provision of peripheral information.

Why solve this

Overcoming a challenge

Due to the exorbitant amount of money required to participate in exhibition events. There is a need for event organizers to convince participating companies that exhibition events are still relevant.

WHAT WE DO

UTILIZATION OF BEACON TECHNOLOGY TO GET CRUCIAL DATA

Deployment of IBeacons

Seamlessly track the whole event venue

By distributing beacons intelligently throughout the event space, information such as the foot traffic, movement patterns, etc., can be retrieved with the involvement of a smartphone application.

Generating useful analytics

Useful insights you can use

With this data, insights like a heatmap of the event can be generated accordingly. The visualised data can then be converted to actionable insights, such as identifying issues like potential resource chokepoints, thereby allowing exhibition event organisers to implement corrective efforts.
